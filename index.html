About Me

Hello! I'm Aron, a passionate cybersecurity enthusiast with a deep interest in Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions and defensive security mechanisms. My journey into the world of cybersecurity began with a fascination for how systems work and, more importantly, how they can be broken and secured.

I thrive on challenges and problem-solving, which is why I'm particularly drawn to areas like web exploitation, reverse engineering, and cryptography. As an active member of the @TriadaCTF team, I continuously hone my skills by collaborating with talented peers and competing in various global competitions. My goal is to leverage my offensive security knowledge to build more robust and resilient systems.
